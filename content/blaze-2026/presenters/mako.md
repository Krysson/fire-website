# Mako(Nevertheless)

I’m a rope educator with roughly 9 years tying and 5 years as an educator, primarily but not exclusively as a top. I’ve helped organize a few local events as well as hosted. I helped Organize Orlando Rope Bite for a couple of years as well as helping organize Rope Study Group which is a curriculum based rope education series. I however consider myself primarily a presenter and feel I excel more in that role. I’ve taught extensively in my local community for Rope Bite as well as Rope Study Group as well as for multiple Rope Conventions across the US. Nationally I’ve taught at Tethered Together, FIRE, SMIRC and Snowbound. I can teach all levels of rope but I feel I am particularly well suited at teaching intermediate and advanced rope both in the realm of technical skill and play/soft skills.
