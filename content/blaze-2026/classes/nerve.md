We all know what it's like to get on someone's nerves, but what about when we're doing rope?
Our nerves are how we interact with our world, through sensation and movement, and a well known risk of rope bondage is injury to these vital organs.
In this class, we'll be going head to toe on the body talking about the nerves that are often encountered and injured in our favorite rope ties. This class will be lecture based, with medical diagrams and human body modeling for examples. Bring your notes books, and get ready to learn about The Nerve of some people!

## Prerequisites

No prior rope experience required. This class is designed for complete beginners.

## What to Bring

- Comfortable clothing that allows movement
- Water bottle
- Notebook for taking notes (optional)
