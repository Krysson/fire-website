This is a space for rope bottoms to gather, share, and learn from each other.

Whether you're exploring what it means to be tied, navigating headspace, figuring out what you actually want, or processing your rope experiences—this is the place. We'll have a facilitator keeping things grounded and moving, but this is your time. Bring the questions that keep you up at night, the things you're curious about, the scenarios you're processing, or just show up ready to listen to others. There's no agenda beyond what this group needs.

This is a judgment-free space where vulnerability and honesty are the whole point. You'll leave with new perspectives, maybe some solid advice, and the reminder that you're not alone in whatever you're figuring out.

## What This Is

- A peer discussion space for rope bottoms
- Facilitated (but not lectured) conversation
- A chance to learn from others' experiences
- A place to ask questions and work through what rope means to you

## Prerequisites

- None, Open to everyone

This is a round table discussion featuring:

Facilitator-guided conversation
Space for whatever bottoms want to talk about
Peer wisdom and real talk
A confidential, judgment-free environment
