This is a space for rope tops to gather, share, and learn from each other.
Whether you're figuring out your tying style, working through the emotional side of topping, navigating consent and communication, troubleshooting technique, or just wanting to connect with other people who tie—this is for you. We'll have a facilitator keeping things on track, but this is your space. Bring the challenges you're facing, the things you're curious about, the wins you want to celebrate, or just show up ready to hear from other tops. No wrong topics.

This is where tops support tops. You'll walk out with fresh ideas, solid advice from people who get it, and the knowledge that whatever you're navigating, you're not doing it alone.

## What This Is

- A peer discussion space for rope tops
- Facilitated (but not lectured) conversation
- A chance to learn from others' experiences and challenges
- A place to troubleshoot, celebrate, and grow as a top

## Prerequisites

- None, Open to everyone

This is a round table discussion featuring:

Facilitator-guided conversation
Space for whatever tops want to talk about
Peer wisdom and real talk
A confidential, judgment-free environment
